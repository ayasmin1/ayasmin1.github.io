The Things I Taste

The things I tasted vary on a wide spectrum. I greatly enjoy eating alot of Italian food, as it’s been something I’ve grown up eating because of my Uncle’s expertise in the area. Beyond this, the taste of South Indian cuisine is one that will stick with me forever: the use of the wide arrays of spices enhances the taste of all things, leaving your mouth to wanting more with every bite I take. Some of the best foods that would be impossible to live without for me are my moms home cooked meals and, my absolute favorite, sushi.
