Importance of Mental Health

Mental health is extremely important, as it can be the underlying factor that many of us miss when facing hardships in our lives. Although it's been discussed in the past, the importance of mental health is much stressed now, as researchers and psychologists have shown compelling evidence that the United States is undergoing a mental health crisis. We have to remember that although our physical selves are important, if our mental health isn't accounted for, we can end up in a horrible place in life. Exposure to mental health issues can help us acknowledge the sensitivity of the case and bring awareness to it.

Statistics on Mental Health in the U.S.

  • ~ Almost half of the adults suffer with a mental illness atleast once in their lifetime
  • ~ The age-of-onset of mental illnesses is 14, but most practioners miss it and only diagnose around the age of 20
  • ~ Women are twice as likely to go through a depressive state that men are (usually due to established gender roles)
  • ~ The most common mental illness is depression, usually alonside with anxiety
