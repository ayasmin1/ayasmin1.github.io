About me

My name's Afrin Yasmin and I'm currently a Junior at Fordham University majoring in Psychology with a minor in Computer Science. I wanted to make this page as an informative place for people to come to get more information on mental health. As somebody who has had issues wiith their mental health in the past, I wanted to make this a place of comfort for people to come to. I aspire to be a clinical psychologist, something that's quite uncommon in my culture. Shining a light on the importance of mental health is a lifelong goal of mine and I hope that this can be a start to something great!
